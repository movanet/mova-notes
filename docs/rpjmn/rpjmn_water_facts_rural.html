RPJMN Water Facts: Rural Water Access and Village Development

Document Type: Source Material (Facts Only)
Source: RPJMN 2025-2029 - National Medium-Term Development Plan
Last Updated: 2025-10-30
Related Analysis: RPJMN_Water_Comprehensive_Analysis

Purpose

This document contains verbatim quotes and statistics regarding rural water access and village development from RPJMN 2025-2029. All content is presented in Indonesian original with English translation side-by-side. This is source material only - no analysis or commentary included.

1. Rural WASH Baseline Statistics

Source: Priority 6 - Building from Villages

Indonesian:

"Secara agregat kualitas layanan dasar perdesaan masih timpang dibandingkan layanan dasar perkotaan, dengan data persentase desa dengan (i) akses air minum aman 18,9 persen; (ii) sanitasi aman 4,77 persen; (iii) layanan kesehatan 25,99 persen; (iv) rumah layak huni 24,62 persen."

English:

"In aggregate, the quality of basic services in rural areas is still unbalanced/imbalanced compared to urban basic services, with data showing the percentage of villages with (i) safe drinking water access at 18.9 percent; (ii) safe sanitation at 4,77 percent; (iii) health services at 25.99 percent; (iv) adequate housing at 24.62 percent."

Rural WASH Statistics:

  • Safe drinking water access: 18.9% of villages (81.1% lack access)
  • Safe sanitation access: 4.77% of villages (95.23% lack access)
  • Adequate housing: 24.62% of villages
  • Health services: 25.99% of villages

2. "Rumah Murah Bersanitasi Baik" - National Priority Program

Source: Priority 6 - Quick Win Program

Affordable Housing with Good Sanitation

Indonesian:

"Melanjutkan Pembangunan Infrastruktur Desa dan Kelurahan, Bantuan Langsung Tunai (BLT), dan Menjamin Penyediaan Rumah Murah Bersanitasi Baik untuk yang Membutuhkan, terutama Generasi Milenial, Generasi Z, dan Masyarakat Berpenghasilan Rendah (MBR)."

English:

"Continuing Village and Neighborhood Infrastructure Development, Direct Cash Assistance (BLT), and Ensuring Provision of Affordable Housing with Good Sanitation for Those in Need, especially Millennials, Generation Z, and Low-Income Communities (MBR)."

Target Population

Indonesian:

  • Masyarakat Berpenghasilan Rendah (MBR) - Low-Income Communities
  • Generasi Milenial dan Generasi Z - Millennials and Generation Z
  • Masyarakat Miskin dan Rentan - Poor and Vulnerable Communities

3. Housing and Sanitation Integration Mandate

Source: Priority 6 - Accelerating Housing Provision

Indonesian:

"Penyediaan perumahan juga perlu dilengkapi dengan infrastruktur dasar permukiman (air minum aman, air limbah, persampahan, drainase lingkungan, dan lainnya)"

English:

"Housing provision also needs to be equipped with basic settlement infrastructure (safe drinking water, wastewater, solid waste, environmental drainage, and others)"

Mandatory Components:
All housing interventions MUST include complete WASH infrastructure.

4. Poor Sanitation as Housing Inadequacy Factor

Source: Priority 6 - Housing Challenges

Indonesian:

"aspek ketahanan bangunan dan kondisi sanitasi yang buruk menjadi faktor utama penyebab ketidaklayakan hunian tersebut."

English:

"building resilience aspects and poor sanitation conditions are the main factors causing housing inadequacy."

National Housing Statistics:

  • Adequate housing (national): 65.25%
  • Adequate housing (rural): 63.83%
  • Adequate housing (urban): 66.26%
  • Inadequate housing: 34.75% of households

Self-Built Housing:

  • 82.47% of houses are self-built
  • 37.10% of self-built houses are inadequate

5. Village Infrastructure Implementation

Source: Priority 6 - Rural Self-Reliance

Dana Desa (Village Fund) Prioritization

Indonesian:

"pembinaan pemerintah desa tentang prioritas pemanfaatan Dana Desa yang digunakan untuk pemenuhan infrastruktur perdesaan"

English:

"guidance for village governments on priorities for utilizing Village Funds used for fulfilling rural infrastructure"

Rural Infrastructure Focus Areas

Indonesian:

"pemenuhan layanan dasar dan infrastruktur, termasuk perumahan dan permukiman, air minum, sanitasi, persampahan, layanan kesehatan, dan konektivitas"

English:

"fulfillment of basic services and infrastructure, including housing and settlements, drinking water, sanitation, solid waste, health services, and connectivity"

6. 30 Priority Rural Development Areas (KPP)

Source: Priority 6 - Rural Economic Development

Indonesian:

"pengembangan pusat pertumbuhan ekonomi lokal, pengembangan skala indikasi 30 Kawasan Perdesaan Prioritas (KPP), penguatan lembaga ekonomi desa"

English:

"development of local economic growth centers, development at the scale of 30 indicated Priority Rural Areas (KPP), strengthening of village economic institutions"

Strategic Focus:
30 Priority Rural Areas receive concentrated infrastructure investment including water and sanitation.

7. Rural-Urban Integration

Source: Priority 6 - Rural-Urban Linkages

Indonesian:

"Pendekatan ini menekankan keterkaitan desa-kota (rural-urban linkages)."

English:

"This approach emphasizes rural-urban linkages."

Purpose:
Infrastructure must support rural-urban economic integration, not just household access.

8. 2045 Universal Access Commitment

Source: Priority 6 - Long-Term Housing Goals

Indonesian:

"pemenuhan 100 persen akses rumah tangga terhadap rumah yang dilengkapi dengan infrastruktur dasar permukiman (air minum aman, air limbah, dan persampahan) pada tahun 2045."

English:

"achieving 100 percent household access to housing equipped with basic settlement infrastructure (safe drinking water, wastewater, and solid waste) by 2045."

Long-Term Vision:
Universal WASH access in housing by 2045 as national commitment.

9. Implementation Mechanisms

Source: Priority 6 - Rural Development Strategy

Mechanisms:

  1. Dana Desa guidance - Village budgets directed toward water/sanitation infrastructure

  2. SEPAKAT system - Integrated data-based development planning for village-level targeting

    • Indonesian: Sistem Perencanaan Pembangunan Berbasis Data Regsosek Terpadu
    • English: Integrated Regsosek Data-Based Development Planning System

  3. Klinik Rumah (Housing Clinics) - Technical assistance for self-build housing with sanitation

  4. Mikro Kredit - Micro credit schemes for incremental construction

  5. Village governments as primary stakeholders - Decentralized implementation

11. Infrastructure Integration Requirements

Source: Priority 6 - Village Infrastructure Strategy

Indonesian:

"penyediaan infrastruktur desa yang terintegrasi dengan infrastruktur supra desa dalam pemenuhan layanan dasar maupun aktivitas ekonomi di desa"

English:

"provision of village infrastructure integrated with supra-village infrastructure in fulfilling basic services and economic activities in villages"

Key Requirements:

  • Integration with supra-village (district/provincial) infrastructure
  • Support for both basic services and economic activities
  • Comprehensive approach to infrastructure development
